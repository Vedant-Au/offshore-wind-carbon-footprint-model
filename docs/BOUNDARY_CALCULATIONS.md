# Boundary and calculation trail

## System boundary

The model is cradle-to-gate: raw-material extraction, processing, component manufacturing, transportation and offshore setup. Operations, maintenance and end-of-life are excluded from the scenario.

## Impact-category contribution

For each reported category:

`manufacturing share = manufacturing / (manufacturing + plant setup)`

This compares stages within the same per-kWh reference table.

## Turbine-level planning scenario

`cradle-to-gate t CO2e = lifecycle t CO2e/MW × capacity MW × cradle-to-gate share`

At 528 t CO2e/MW and 15 MW, the lifecycle planning total is 7,920 t CO2e. Applying 70%-80% gives 5,544-6,336 t CO2e.

These are two separate analytical views. They are not summed.

## Management rule

Prioritise stages with the greatest contribution, then validate technical feasibility, cost and supplier influence. Direction is defensible; product-level precision requires a verified inventory.
